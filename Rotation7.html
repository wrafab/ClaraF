Rotation 7:Soldering, Electronics & Arduino


Electronics: OHM's Law, Circuits


In class today, we learn the basic knowledge about electric circuit----the OHM's Law which basically means the voltage across an conductor is directly propotionally to the current through it. We also learn the symbols for resistors, LEDs, switches, capacitors and the other things of the electric circuit.




Soldering

I turned on the the soldering station first and it would heat up in a few seconds. Arduino IDE Projects

In the class, we needed to work on our Arduino IDE projects. There are 15 different circuits that we needed to finish in total. We need to finish these arduino circuits according to the circuits diagram. Making such Arduino circuits is hard. For such circuits, if I mess up a single wires or a single resistor, the whole circuit would not work. It's a painful process. However, overall I enjoyed the process of making these circuits because they gave me the sense of satisfaction.
